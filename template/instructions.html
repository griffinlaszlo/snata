How to request your data from Snapchat:


Step 1: Log into https://accounts.snapchat.com


Step 2: Click on 'MY DATA'




Step 3: Scroll to the bottom of this page




Step 4: Unselect option to filter export by date range




Step 5: Enter your Snapchat email address and click Submit Request






Once you have submitted your request, you will receive an email from Snapchat with a link to download your data.

It should look like this:




Next, download your .zip file and upload it to SNATA!
